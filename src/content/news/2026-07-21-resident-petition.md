A group of Ladera Ranch residents, including members of Ladera Health Watch, have started a petition on Change.org asking LARMAC to suspend certain pesticide products in common areas and adopt further Integrated Pest Management practices.

We're sharing this petition because residents within our group asked us to, not because Ladera Health Watch has independently verified every claim it makes. As with everything else on this site: no cancer cluster has been officially determined in Ladera Ranch, and there is no known environmental cause of Ewing sarcoma. The petition is one way some residents are choosing to advocate. Signing it, like everything here, is entirely voluntary.
